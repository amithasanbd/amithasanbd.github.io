Professional Profile

Early-career oceanography researcher with hands-on experience in environmental research, fieldwork, data analytics, geospatial applications and technical communication. I am in the final stage of an M.Sc. in Oceanography at Bangladesh Maritime University, with only the thesis semester remaining, and I hold a B.Sc. in Oceanography.

My focus areas include water quality, coastal ecosystem health, remote sensing and GIS, marine heatwaves, PFZ modelling, blue economy, and machine learning for Earth and ocean applications.

Education

  • M.Sc. in Oceanography — Bangladesh Maritime University, expected Dec 2026
  • B.Sc. in Oceanography — Bangladesh Maritime University, 2025, CGPA 3.55/4.00

Career Objective

To contribute to Marine AI, data analytics, environmental modelling, GIS/remote sensing and Digital Twin Ocean research while preparing for a fully funded PhD abroad.

Research Philosophy

I believe ocean science should combine field evidence, satellite observation, data analytics, modelling and clear communication to support sustainable coastal and marine decisions.
